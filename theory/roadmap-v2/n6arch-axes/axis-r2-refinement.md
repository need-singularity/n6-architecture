## §1. Task A — X1 NUM-CORE ↔ X5 LATTICE-VOA Merger Experiment

### 1.1 Background

In the R1 overlap matrix, X1 ↔ X5 = **7** (strongest sharing). Core grounds:

- Ramanujan Δ = Dedekind η^{J₂} (weight σ) is both X1's SEED-20 anchor and X5's SEED-12 anchor
- Moonshine VOA V^♮ central charge c = 24 = J₂ = σ·φ = n·τ is a direct expression of Theorem 0
- K3 χ = J₂ (X5's SEED-13) has a modular relation with η^{24}

### 1.2 Pro-merger Grounds (≥5)

1. **Common mathematical infrastructure**: modular forms (weight σ cusp form), VOA, lattice theta functions form **one category**. Since Eichler-Zagier (1985), Borcherds (1992), the unified theory has matured.
2. **Single Theorem 0 anchor**: σ·φ = n·τ = J₂ = 24 interprets `(modular form weight = σ)`, `(VOA central charge = J₂)`, `(K3 χ = J₂)` all as **one arithmetic identity**. Merging unifies the anchor.
3. **BT coverage overlap**: both X1 and X5 partially contribute to BT-545 (Hodge K3) and BT-541 (RH modular). Merging dissolves the "which axis owns BT-545?" ambiguity.
4. **Shared evidence files**: `bt-1392 §1.5` (K3 Hodge diamond), `bt-1407 §1.1` (Moonshine c=24), `papers/moonshine-barrier-honest-report.md` are common evidence to both axes.
5. **Naturalness of merged axis name**: MODULAR-ANCHOR as "modular anchor" simultaneously contributes to RH + Hodge + BSD among the 7 problems (common substrate of Mazur 1977, Wiles 1995, Katz-Sarnak 1999).
6. **Hexa-verification consolidation**: `verify_theorem_b.hexa` (Bernoulli) and `verify_moonshine_c24.hexa` would consolidate into `verify_modular_anchor.hexa`, eliminating code duplication.
7. **R1 evidence-structure reinforcement**: X1 has 7 components + X5 has 8 = 15; merged set could shrink to 10-12 (deduplication). Advantage density increases.

### 1.3 Anti-merger Grounds (≥5)

1. **Independence of the Moonshine BARRIER**: `moonshine-barrier-honest-report-2026-04-15.md` formally declares that the n=6 coordinate necessity of V^♮ is **not yet a candidate argument**. This is **a separate-dimension hard problem** from the Theorem B / Bernoulli pathway. Merging would dilute the BARRIER into "a weakness inside NUM-CORE".
2. **Heterogeneity of proof pathways**: X1's core tools are **analytic number theory** (ζ, L-functions, Bernoulli, η, Hecke), while X5's core is **infinite-dimensional algebra** (VOA, lattices, Monster representations). The mathematical-rigor candidate-argument machinery is wholly different.
3. **Primary-axis problem for BT-545 Hodge**: X5 is the only strength-9 axis for BT-545. After merging, MODULAR-ANCHOR's BT-545 strength would still be 9, but it would carry "split attention" along with BT-541, dispersing R2 promotion drill-down.
4. **Evaluation of LATTICE-VOA droppability**: X5 score 3.6 is low, but it is the only axis that needs "retention" rather than "drop". Merging risks being a "hidden drop".
5. **Heterogeneity in core-component count**: of X1's 7 components, 0 are lattice-based; of X5's 8 components, 0 are Bernoulli-based. **Intersection** is the single Ramanujan Δ = η^{J₂} component. The remaining 14 components would coexist awkwardly after merging.
6. **Evidence requires independent verification**: X1 rests on a rigorous Theorem B candidate argument; X5 rests on Moonshine and the independent CKM-R-V 2017 result. Merged, if either of "two grounds" breaks, the entire axis weakens. Keeping them separated secures **stability**.
7. **Distinction as engineering axes**: NUM-CORE is "the attack vector against number-theoretic BTs"; LATTICE-VOA is "the attack vector against Hodge / VOA BTs". Merged, the decision "which BT to invest in first" becomes blurred.

### 1.4 Merger Decision — **partial integration** (hybrid)

**Verdict basis**:
- 7 pro, 7 con. Almost balanced.
- Decisive factor: **independent reality of the Moonshine BARRIER**. `moonshine-barrier-honest-report` is already "separately analyzed" in this project and connects to the Red Team pathway of X7 HONEST-HARNESS. Pushing the BARRIER into "a weakness inside NUM-CORE" risks violating the honesty principle.

**Final decision**: **do not merge**, but assign the **shared region (Δ=η^{J₂})** exclusively to X1, and reduce-redefine X5 to "VOA + lattice + Moonshine BARRIER". The name is kept while some content is transferred.

**Transfer contents**:
- X5 → X1 transfer: SEED-20 Δ=η^{J₂} anchor (currently both sides)
- Removed from X1 → X5: lattice theta functions, K3-VOA connection (X5-exclusive)
- X5 core redefinition: **V^♮ Monster representation + Leech lattice triple-extremality (CKM-R-V 2017) + Kervaire-Milnor Θ**

This makes X5 abbreviatable as **MOONSHINE-LATTICE** (name LATTICE-VOA retained).

**Post-merger score forecast**: X1 advantage 9.2 → **9.5** (Δ=η^{J₂} clarified), X5 3.6 → **4.0** (narrowed scope, density increased). These numbers are recomputed in §4.
