Grading scale:
- **EXACT**: The claimed number/structure matches real-world data precisely (<0.1% for continuous quantities, exact for integers/theorems), with a legitimate physical or mathematical basis.

- **FAIL**: Contradicted by data, unit-dependent, or no clean formula found.
- **UNVERIFIABLE**: Insufficient published data to confirm or deny.
