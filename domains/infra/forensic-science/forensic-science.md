# 법과학 (HEXA-FORENSIC) — n=6 Locard 6 trace evidence classes

## §1 WHY

Locard's exchange principle anchors **6 trace-evidence classes** (fiber,
glass, hair, soil, paint, DNA / biological). FBI CODIS forensic DNA STR
analysis originally standardised on **6 core loci** (later expanded to 20;
the 6-locus baseline is still the reference). Galton fingerprint
minutiae: **6 feature types** (ridge ending, bifurcation, short ridge,
enclosure, dot, spur).

## §2 n=6 CONNECTION

- **6 Locard trace classes** — evidence-collection primitive.
- **6 CODIS core loci** — original DNA profiling baseline.
- **6 Galton minutiae types** — fingerprint classification primitive.

## §3 DIFFERENTIATION

Distinct from `law` (legal procedure) and `bio-pharma` (drug dev). Forensic
science is the trace-evidence / identification discipline.

## §4 SOURCES

- Locard 1930, *L'Enquête criminelle et les méthodes scientifiques*.
- FBI CODIS standard (original 6-locus).
- Galton 1892, *Finger Prints*.
