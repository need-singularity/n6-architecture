# H-CX-483: Egyptian Fraction = GUT Coupling Unification

> **Hypothesis**: The ratio α₁:α₂:α₃ matches {1/2, 1/3, 1/6} at some energy scale.

## Grade: ⚪ REJECTED

## Results

SM one-loop running from M_Z to 10¹⁹ GeV: at NO scale do the coupling fractions match {1/2, 1/3, 1/6}.

```
At M_Z:        α₁:α₂:α₃ ≈ {0.10, 0.20, 0.70}
At 10⁶ GeV:    best match, RMS deviation 0.029 (poor)
At 10¹⁷ GeV:   couplings approach {1/3, 1/3, 1/3} (equal fractions)
```

The three couplings converge toward equality, not toward the Egyptian fraction pattern. The Egyptian fraction identity 1/2+1/3+1/6=1 does not describe gauge coupling unification under SM running.

## Status: Rejected — Egyptian fraction has no GUT coupling realization
