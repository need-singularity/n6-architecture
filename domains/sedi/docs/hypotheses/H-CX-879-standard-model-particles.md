# H-CX-879: Standard Model Particles — 12 Fermions + 5 Bosons = σ + sopfr = 17

> **Hypothesis**: The Standard Model has 6 quarks + 6 leptons + 4 gauge bosons + 1 Higgs = 17 = σ + sopfr fundamental particles. The fermion sector has σ = 12 particles. The boson sector has sopfr = 5 particles.
