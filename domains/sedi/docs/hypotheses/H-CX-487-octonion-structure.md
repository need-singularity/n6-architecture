# H-CX-487: 8 Mathematical Domains = Octonion Structure

> **Hypothesis**: The 8 domains form an octonion-like (Fano plane) multiplication structure.

## Grade: ⚪ REJECTED

## Results

The domain co-occurrence pattern does NOT match the Fano plane:

- **Fano requires**: 7 triples, every point on exactly 3 lines, symmetric cyclic structure
- **Observed**: 6 triples (close but not 7), hub-dominated hierarchy (A, N, I are hubs)
- **S is isolated**: 0 connections, making Fano impossible
- **Structure is hierarchical**, not the non-associative cyclic symmetry of octonions

The 8 domains have a star/hub topology centered on {A, N, I}, not the symmetric structure required by octonion algebra.

## Status: Rejected — hub-dominated, not Fano plane
