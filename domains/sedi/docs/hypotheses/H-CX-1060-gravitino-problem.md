### Physical Context

In supersymmetric cosmology, gravitinos produced thermally in the early universe can overclose it or spoil Big Bang nucleosynthesis through late decays. The gravitino problem constrains either the reheating temperature or the gravitino mass itself. Heavy gravitinos (>10 TeV) decay quickly enough to avoid BBN constraints. Light gravitinos (<1 keV) have negligible energy density. The forbidden window between these bounds creates tension between SUSY model building and cosmology.

### Texas Sharpshooter Check
