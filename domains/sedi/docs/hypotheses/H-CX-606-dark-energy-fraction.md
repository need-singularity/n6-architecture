### n=6 Decomposition

```
Standalone:
  7  = M₃ = 2³-1             → third Mersenne prime
  10 = σ-φ = 12-2            → abundance minus totient

Closed system:
  13 = σ+R(6) = 12+1         → abundance + fixed point
```

### The 60/86 Form

```
σ·sopfr = 12×5 = 60          → number of inflation e-folds (H-CX-615)
P₂-φ = 28-2 = 26            → d_bosonic
σ·sopfr + P₂-φ = 60+26 = 86

Ω_Λ = (inflation e-folds) / (e-folds + d_bosonic) = 60/86
```

This interpretation links dark energy to the ratio of inflationary expansion to string-theoretic dimensionality.

### Physical Interpretation

Dark energy dominates the universe at a fraction set by the Mersenne prime M₃ divided by the excess of divisor sum over totient. The closed-system value 13/19 remains the gold standard at 0.07%.
