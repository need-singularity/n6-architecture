### Physical Context

Apoptosis (programmed cell death) is essential for development, immune function, and tumor suppression. The intrinsic/extrinsic pathway distinction was established through work by Bhatt et al. and others in the 1990s-2000s. Both pathways converge on the effector caspases (especially caspase-3), forming a common execution module. Dysregulation of apoptosis underlies cancer and autoimmune diseases.

### Texas Sharpshooter Check

The φ = 2 pathways are universally recognized. The caspase classification (4 initiator + 3 effector = 7) is standard in biochemistry textbooks. Some sources include caspase-12 or -14, but these have specialized roles. The M₃ = 7 total for core apoptotic caspases is robust.
