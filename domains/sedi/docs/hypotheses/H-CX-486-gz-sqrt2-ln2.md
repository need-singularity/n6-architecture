# H-CX-486: GZ_width / (√2-1) ≈ ln(2)

> **Hypothesis**: Golden Zone width divided by the quantum-classical gap equals the information unit.

## Grade: ⚪ NOT SIGNIFICANT

## Results

```
ln(4/3) / (√2 - 1) = 0.28768 / 0.41421 = 0.6945
ln(2) = 0.6931
Error: 0.20%
```

Rearranged: ln(4/3)/ln(2) = 0.41504 ≈ √2-1 = 0.41421 (same identity, 0.20%).

### Monte Carlo

1 match from 1320 triples of 12 famous constants at 0.2% threshold. Expected by chance: ~2. Not significant.

### Interpretation

Suggestive as a n=6 connection (GZ = ln(τ/3), √2 = √(σ/s), ln2 = ln(φ)), but numerically indistinguishable from coincidence.
