# H-CX-514: Fermion Mass Ratios as 2^(p/q) with n=6 Denominators

> **Hypothesis**: Fermion mass ratios are powers of 2 with n=6 rational exponents.

## Grade: ⚪ NOT SIGNIFICANT

## Results

- 31/36 mass ratios (86%) match n=6 rationals within 1%
- Random baseline: 29.5/36 (82%)
- No statistically significant excess

The n=6 rational set is too dense (399 values in [0,25)) for this test to discriminate. The 1% tolerance window exceeds the average gap between rationals, so random masses also match at high rates.

## Status: Rejected — n=6 rational density too high for discrimination
