### Physical Context

The Debye temperature Θ_D marks the transition between quantum and classical behavior of lattice vibrations (phonons). Below Θ_D, quantum effects dominate and C_v ∝ T³. Above Θ_D, the classical Dulong-Petit limit C_v = 3Nk applies.

For iron (Fe, Z=26):
- Θ_D = 470 K is well-established experimentally
- Iron is the most abundant element by mass in Earth's core
- Z = 26 = P₂ - φ = 28 - 2 (noted for completeness)
