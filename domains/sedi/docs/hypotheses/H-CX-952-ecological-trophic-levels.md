### Physical Context

Lindeman's 10% rule (1942) established that roughly 10% of energy transfers between trophic levels. This is an approximation — real values range from 5-20% — but 10% is the canonical figure used universally in ecology textbooks. The τ = 4 trophic level limit emerges naturally from this exponential decay.
