The seven-band classification is conventional but physically motivated by distinct generation/detection mechanisms: radio (oscillating currents), microwave (molecular rotation), IR (molecular vibration), visible (electronic transitions in outer shells), UV (higher electronic transitions), X-ray (inner shell/bremsstrahlung), gamma (nuclear transitions).
