### Physical Context

The latitudinal biodiversity gradient is one of ecology's oldest recognized patterns (Humboldt, 1807). Axial tilt is the primary driver of seasonality, which in turn creates the latitudinal climate gradient. The current obliquity of 23.44° oscillates between 22.1° and 24.5° over a 41,000-year Milankovitch cycle. The TECS-L value 23.5° falls within this range and matches the current value to 0.26%.

### Texas Sharpshooter Check
