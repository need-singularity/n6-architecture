# H-CX-550: CHSH Quantum Probability = (φ+√φ)/τ — Exact

> **Hypothesis**: The optimal quantum success probability in the CHSH game is (φ(6)+√φ(6))/τ(6) = (2+√2)/4.

## Grade: 🟦 PROVEN (exact algebraic identity)

## Results

```
P_quantum = cos²(π/8) = (2+√2)/4 = (φ+√φ)/τ      EXACT

P_classical = 3/4 = (σ/τ)/τ                         EXACT

Quantum advantage = P_Q/P_C = (φ+√φ)/(σ/τ) = (2+√2)/3
```

### Significance

The CHSH game is the foundational test of quantum nonlocality (Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt 1969; aspect, Nobel 2022). That both the classical (3/4) and quantum ((2+√2)/4) optimal strategies are n=6 expressions connects quantum foundations to number theory.
