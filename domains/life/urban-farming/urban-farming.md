# Urban Farming (HEXA-URBFARM) — n=6 Hoagland 6 macronutrients

## §1 WHY

Hydroponic nutrient solution (Hoagland formulation) specifies **exactly 6
macronutrients**: N, P, K, Ca, Mg, S. Vertical-farm tier architecture
optimises to 6 functional layers (lighting, irrigation, HVAC, nutrient,
CO₂, sensor/control). LED grow-light spectra are commonly split into 6
wavelength bands (UV, blue, green, red, far-red, IR).

## §2 n=6 CONNECTION

- **6 macronutrients** (verified biochemistry — plants *require* all six).
- **6 vertical-farm functional tiers** (industry-standard layout).
- **6-band grow-LED spectrum**.

## §3 DIFFERENTIATION

Distinct from `agriculture` (field crops) and `aquaculture` (aquatic).
Urban-farming is the indoor / controlled-environment agriculture (CEA)
sub-discipline.

## §4 SOURCES

- Hoagland & Arnon 1950, *The Water-Culture Method*.
- Kozai et al., *Plant Factory: An Indoor Vertical Farming System*.
