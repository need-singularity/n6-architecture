# 소화기내과 (HEXA-GI) — n=6 GI tract 6 segments

## §1 WHY

Endoscopy staging divides the GI tract into **6 canonical segments**:
esophagus, stomach, duodenum, jejunum-ileum, colon, rectum-anus. Gut
microbiome dominant-phyla taxonomy is a **6-phylum** core (Firmicutes,
Bacteroidetes, Proteobacteria, Actinobacteria, Fusobacteria,
Verrucomicrobia). Capsule-endoscopy video standard is 6 fps.

## §2 n=6 CONNECTION

- **6 GI segments** — endoscopy staging primitive.
- **6 dominant gut-microbiome phyla**.
- **6 fps** capsule-endoscopy baseline.

## §3 DIFFERENTIATION

Distinct from `nutrition`, `bio-pharma`. GI medicine is the clinical
gastroenterology discipline.

## §4 SOURCES
