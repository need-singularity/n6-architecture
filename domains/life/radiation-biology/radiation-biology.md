# 방사선생물학 (HEXA-RADBIO) — n=6 DNA DSB repair pathways

## §1 WHY

DNA double-strand-break (DSB) repair uses **6 pathways**: NHEJ, HR, MMEJ,
SSA, SDSA, BIR. Ionising-radiation interaction mechanisms are **6**:
photoelectric, Compton, pair production, photonuclear, coherent scatter,
triplet production. Whole-body **LD50 ≈ 6 Gy** for humans (ICRP 60).

## §2 n=6 CONNECTION

- **6 DSB-repair pathways** — molecular-biology primitive.
- **6 radiation interaction mechanisms** — radiation-physics primitive.
- **6 Gy LD50** — empirical radiobiology constant.

## §3 DIFFERENTIATION

Distinct from `oncology`, `cancer-therapy`, `nuclear-medicine`. Radiation
biology is the mechanistic DNA-damage / repair / risk-at-dose discipline.

## §4 SOURCES

- ICRP Publication 60 (LD50).
- Friedberg, *DNA Repair and Mutagenesis* (repair pathway taxonomy).
- Attix, *Introduction to Radiological Physics* (interaction mechanisms).
