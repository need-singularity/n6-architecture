# 민족음악학 (HEXA-ETHNOMUSIC) — n=6 hexatonic scales

## §1 WHY

The **hexatonic blues scale (6 notes: root, m3, 4, b5, 5, m7)** is the
dominant popular-music scale globally. Pentatonic (5-note) systems plus a
tonic form 6-note hexatonic variants as a cross-cultural universal. Nettl's
classification of world-music systems identifies **6 modal archetypes**.

## §2 n=6 CONNECTION

- **6-note hexatonic blues scale** — globally dominant.
- **6 modal archetypes** (Nettl).
- **Pentatonic + tonic → 6** — cross-cultural convergent count.

## §3 DIFFERENTIATION

Distinct from `music` (12-tone Western practice) and `music-mathematics`
(abstract tuning theory). Ethnomusicology is the comparative-cultural music
discipline.

## §4 SOURCES

- Nettl, *The Study of Ethnomusicology: Thirty-Three Discussions*.
- Brown 1991, on pentatonic universality.
- Hexatonic blues scale (music-theory standard reference).
