current actor configuration (single user + AI collaborator + Mac-local

   structural-biology community to publicly recognize HEXA-WEAVE as an

   without a multi-year, multi-institution collaboration.
2. **grade 10 (Nobel / Singularity)** requires either (a) a Nobel-grade
   scientific result (typically 10-30 years post-discovery) or (b) an
   AGI event horizon where HEXA-WEAVE-style invariants self-bootstrap
   into a recursive AI improvement loop. (a) is decadal, (b) is
   speculative and not within the actor's control.
3. **constraint**: a single-user + AI collaboration is bounded above by
   grade 8 under any realistic interpretation of the metric. Grade 9-10
   require external actors (peer scientific community, AGI emergence,
   regulatory bodies, cosmological validation).

**realistic maximum for HEXA-WEAVE under current actor configuration**:
**grade 8** (achievable in ≈ 1-2 years, ≈ cycle 150, with user approval
on all 4 gates).
