## §2 주요 정리

```lean
theorem theorem_B_prime_power {p : ℕ} (hp : p.Prime) {a : ℕ} (ha : a ≥ 2) :
    σ 1 (p ^ a) * Nat.totient (p ^ a) ≠ (p ^ a) * (Nat.divisors (p ^ a)).card
```

**보조 lemmas (모두 sorry 없음)**:
- `two_pow_ge_lin`: $2^a \geq a+2$ for $a \geq 2$ (Nat.le_induction from base 2)
- `prime_pow_ge_linear`: $p^{a+1} \geq p(a+2)$ for $p \geq 2, a \geq 2$
- `prime_pow_strict_gt`: $p^{a+1} > p(a+1) + 1$ for $p \geq 2, a \geq 2$
- `tau_prime_pow`: $\tau(p^a) = a + 1$ via `sigma_zero_apply_prime_pow`
- `sigma_one_prime_pow_sum`: $\sigma(p^a) = \sum_{k=0}^{a} p^k$
- `geom_sum_identity`: $(p-1) \cdot \sum_{k=0}^{a} p^k = p^{a+1} - 1$ (induction on $a$)

## §3 증명 핵심

**수작업 유도**:
- $\sigma(p^a) = 1 + p + p^2 + \cdots + p^a$ (Mathlib sum form)
- $\varphi(p^a) = p^{a-1}(p-1)$ (Nat.totient_prime_pow)
- $\tau(p^a) = a + 1$

$$\sigma(p^a) \cdot \varphi(p^a) = \left(\sum_{k=0}^{a} p^k\right) \cdot p^{a-1}(p-1) = p^{a-1} \cdot (p^{a+1} - 1)$$

(마지막 등식: geom_sum_identity 에서 $(p-1) \sum p^k = p^{a+1} - 1$)

$p^a \cdot \tau(p^a) = p^a \cdot (a+1) = p \cdot p^{a-1} \cdot (a+1)$

등식:
$$p^{a-1} \cdot (p^{a+1} - 1) = p \cdot p^{a-1} \cdot (a+1)$$
$p^{a-1} > 0$ 양변 나누기:
$$p^{a+1} - 1 = p(a+1)$$
즉 $p^{a+1} = p(a+1) + 1$.

**`prime_pow_strict_gt`** 에 의해 $p^{a+1} > p(a+1) + 1$, 모순.
